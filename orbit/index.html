Orbit

Orbit is a game about space and gravity. You have to shoot your Spaceship across space to get back to earth. You will learn to use the gravitation of planets to your advantage. But watch out for black holes and meteorites, as they might interfere with your way home.

In Orbit you can get creative. There are many known and even more unknown solutions for each level.
